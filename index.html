Laser Tap.

Gameplay

"Laser Tap" is an addictive IOS game. You must use quick reflexes and a sharp eye to find a safe pocket before the lasers catch you.

Highscore

"Laser Tap" can be quite daunting at first, but over time you will see an improvement in your highscore. At first you may be happy surviving 10 lasers, but in just a few minutes you will be getting past more than 25!

Future Updates

"Laser Tap" is a solid game on its own, but there is still plenty of room for improvements.
